The Dance

Beat Zoo is a dance event where you can blow the dust off your essence and have a rendezvous with the real you.

We surf a musical wave, from 0 to 1 in intensity, and back to 0 again. Arrive as you are, have a sweaty romp, and leave restored.

The DJ

Ed Terry’s fingers have danced over piano keys since before his legs could reach the pedals. This was the only expressive movement he knew until he met the surge of a subwoofer and the shimmer of hi-hats, and his whole body was suddenly in play.

On his dance floors, he invites you to pass up perfection, and instead delight in the full spectrum of movement, from glitching to gliding.

His eclectic style stamps your passport with House, Afro, Latin and Bass notes to dislodge the familiar and bring what’s just out of reach into your grasp.

Ed has played at Burning Man and at Ecstatic Dances in five cities around the US.
